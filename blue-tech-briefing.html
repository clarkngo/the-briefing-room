Strategic Partnership: City University of Seattle & Washington Maritime Blue

Strategic Opportunity: Academic Membership

By joining Washington Maritime Blue as a non-profit academic member, CityU STC positions itself at the center of Seattle's $30B maritime technology cluster. This provides direct influence over the Maritime Innovation Center (MInC), which is the flagship physical home of Maritime Blue innovation.

Physical AI: Machines that sense and act in the physical world—the next step after LLMs.
Cluster Synergy: Maritime Blue manages the projects that the Innovation Center hosts.
