Strategic Briefing: Blue Tech Ecosystem

Empowering the School of Technology and Computing for 2026 and Beyond

What are we talking about?

Blue Tech: The application of advanced technology (AI, robotics, data) to the ocean economy—focusing on sustainability, shipping, and offshore energy.

Physical AI: Unlike digital AI (chatbots), Physical AI allows machines to perceive, reason, and act in the physical world. Think autonomous boats or robotic sensors in the Puget Sound.
